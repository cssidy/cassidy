Credentials & Biography

Hello, I’m Cassidy.

I have an original mind bent on solving real problems. I employ design principles and thoughtful code to build seamless experiences on the web.

Besides telling computers what to do, I’m good at doing what computers have a hard time with. Diverse high-level thinking, cross-discipline synthesis, reasoning with context, and making decisions that involve value systems and ethics. I enjoy thinking about the philosophy behind what humans do.

I am based in the small, idyllic city of Burlington, VT. When I’m not building web apps, I’m often engaged in art and music, or researching some topic that’s captured my interest.

What types of web apps do I make?

I want to build systems that improve the human experience without harming the planet. I always consider the ethical consequences of new technology. These are some areas that I care about:

  • Renewable energy
  • Resource management
  • Waste and recycling
  • Overpopulation
  • Environmental systems
  • Corruption

I want to find ways for humankind to exist complimentary to nature, yet enjoy the comforts of modern technology. I imagine a world where the human species is sustainable without comprising the planet and other species. I think this is possible if we put our minds to it.
